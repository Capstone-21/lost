About US

This is the unofficial Lost & Found management system for the University of Oklahoma. This digital system for storing and searching lost and found items has been developed by members of the Class of 2021 Computer Science Capstone class. We hope to make finding lost items on campus quick and easy for students

FAQs

Q: How can I find my lost items?

A: Try searching by item name or description (airpod, textbook, etc.) or using our search filters (date lost, location lost, color, etc.).

Q: Where do I go to pick up my items?

A: The item posting will list the drop off location.
